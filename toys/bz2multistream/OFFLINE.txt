Offline readers for Wikipedia or other Wikimedia projects known
to the author of this list as of September 2012

Some of these are Free Software and some aren't; also the list is
bound to be pretty incomplete.  If you know of other (Free Software)
apps, feel free to drop a line to the maintainer, currently ariel at
wikimedia.org.  At some point if the list gets too large we'll just
list the most recent Free apps.

aarddict -- cross-platform (desktop)
http://aarddict.org/

allofwiki offline -- iOS
http://www.brilliantish.com/allofwiki/

BzReader -- windows
http://code.google.com/p/bzreader/

evopedia -- linux, windows
http://evopedia.info/

Kiwix -- cross-plaform (desktop)
http://sourceforge.net/projects/kiwix/

MzReader -- windows
http://homepage.ntlworld.com/bharat.vadera/MzReader/

http://code.google.com/p/offline-wiki/
